About Jake Groves

Jake has played harp with several bands in the Louisville area, including Bottom Sop, Tyler Lance Walker Gill, John Clay and the Boxwine Prophets, Brent Mathis, Spooky Diamonds, Crittenden & the Free Estate and his own project Jack Rabbit and the Dirty Hares. Jake also recently joined rising folk/country star Colter Wall's band and has been touring the nation with his harmonicas, playing everywhere from Stagecoach to the Ryman auditorium.

He continues to study with Howard and other harmonica gurus such as Grant Dermody and is happy to pass on the knowledge he has gained to aspiring players.

Book Jake

Jake is available for harmonica recording/live performance, as well as individual/group lessons.
